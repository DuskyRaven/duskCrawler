The Sky Today,
January 3

By Nyssa Grazda

At 4:15 a.m.: The moon is sextile Venus

In the morning, the Libra moon sextiles Venus in Sagittarius, encouraging us to share our feelings. Honest discussions improve relationship rapport and help us to bond. This is an ideal time to take in entertainment, attend a social gathering, or go on a day trip with loved ones. Introductions between family and friends should be light-hearted and harmonious. Our charming and peaceable nature supports meeting new people or going on dates. We maintain confidence in how we present ourselves and our convictions, even if we wander into divisive conversational territory.

At 7:30 p.m.: The moon is square the sun

The cosmic weather is then quiet until after dark, when the last quarter moon in Libra invites us to reflect upon events that occurred during the December 26 Cancer full moon. At that time, we were advised to fill our own cup and tend to our emotional well-being. If we hoped to achieve success in the wider world, putting our Selves on the backburner was not an option. Yet today, we have no tolerance for our own shortcomings. We’re frustrated with our lack of quantifiable “progress” in our initiatives, and we’d rather gloss over the uncomfortable emotions we’re experiencing and distract ourselves from our assumed deficiencies. We may even be upset that we haven’t immediately made strides towards our New Year’s resolutions. We’re only three days into 2024, and Mercury has just stationed direct! Cut yourself some slack and acknowledge that Rome wasn’t built in a day.
